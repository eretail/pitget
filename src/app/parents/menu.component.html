Menu

It is our goal to provide lunch options that are both delicious and healthy. Parents can order lunches through The Healthy Lunch Box. All meals include an entrée, a side, a dessert and milk. A vegetarian option is also available daily. Parents in ECP can sign up for lunch service for the semester or the year. Parents in K-8th grades can order lunch daily through The Healthy Lunch Box online ordering system.
