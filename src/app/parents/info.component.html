Children’s Health

Students who are ill and/or have a fever must remain at home. The regulations of the Texas Department of Health require that all students must be free from fever (100.4° F or higher) for more than 24 hours before returning to school. The School Nurse will decide whether or not a child is well enough to attend class.  The School Nurse’s decision is final in these matters.

Emergency Closings

Please note that The Honor Roll School will follow the emergency closure of Fort Bend Independent School District. You can go to www.click2houston.com or follow the local TV channels to get information on school closings. The school will also send out text messages via JupiterEd, notifying parents about emergency closings. Please ensure your JupiterEd settings allow for text messaging under each parent’s account.
