 Summer Camp

Our summer campers (ages 5-14) have the opportunity to expand their horizons, embark on new adventures, and have fun. Our exceptional program and dedicated staff create an environment that forges lifelong friendships among our campers. We create a summer experience that is unforgettable! Camp activities include sports & games, arts & crafts, science & cooking, songs & skits, field trips and swimming.
