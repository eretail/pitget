About Us

Dear Parents:

Welcome to PiageT Academy. We are a private, nonsectarian, coeducational day school in Norcross that provides an enriched and highly challenging education for all of our students. We are constantly invested in professional development and curriculum to ensure that THRS meets the highest standards for early childhood through eighth grade education. Our school serves over 600 students who reside in the local community and originate from dozens of countries around the world.

Our curriculum emphasizes the knowledge and skills that students need to be successful in the 21st century: mastery of core content, strong study habits, effective communication, personal organization and full understanding of technology and its benefits. We build the skills of self-confidence and achievement to enable our graduates to enroll and succeed in the high school of their choice.

We are tremendously proud of our reputation for providing high quality education. There are many reasons for our ongoing success, including the high caliber of our students and faculty and the support of our parents. We are all deeply committed to instilling a passion for excellence in our students, and our graduates exemplify our success.

In addition to academic excellence, we emphasize the values of honor, respect and responsibility. We expect students to be understanding of individual differences, to treat peers, teachers and others with respect, and to pursue the ideals of personal integrity, initiative and diligence. The acceptance of these values among our students has allowed us to create a safe, caring and trusting environment in which they are able to grow and learn.

Our teachers are passionate, supportive and dedicated individuals who use their creativity and sensitivity to bring our well-rounded curriculum to life. They personalize lessons to ensure that instruction is stimulating and challenging for each student. Our instructors were chosen not only for their expertise in teaching, but also for their ability to model the values we have espoused.
